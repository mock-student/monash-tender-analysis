1. Overview

The Mentorship module connects NTU students (mentees) with alumni and industry professionals (mentors) through structured mentorship programmes. Mentees browse and connect with approved mentors; mentors accept connections and participate in admin-created programmes. Admins manage programme setup, mentor approvals, matching oversight, and programme outcome reporting.

Scope note — Mentee access: The Mentorship module supports existing NTU students as mentees only, accessed via NTU SSO. There is no requirement to capture peer mentoring (student-to-student) within the system. Confirmed by Penny (NTU CAO), 12 June 2026.

Users: Mentees (students via SSO), Mentors (alumni + industry professionals), Admins (L4).

4. Prototype Scope for Vetrick

Key screens and flows to sketch for CP4 discovery:

  1. Mentee registration and profile — SSO sign-in → profile completion (SIS auto-fill + editable fields, resume upload, career interests; Video CV if in scope)
  1. Mentor registration and profile — self-registration form (alumni vs. industry professional distinction), profile editing, awaiting-approval state
  1. Admin: mentor approval dashboard — pending applications list, profile view, approve/reject with reason
  1. Mentor search and connection flow — mentee searches/filters approved mentors → views profile → sends connection request; mentor receives, views mentee profile, accepts/declines
  1. Programme feed — upcoming programmes listing (mentor and mentee views), register CTA, visibility-restricted view
  1. AI-recommended mentors — mentee home state: "Recommended for you" based on course/interests/skills
  1. Admin: programme and matching dashboard — real-time metrics, report generation, outcome tracking

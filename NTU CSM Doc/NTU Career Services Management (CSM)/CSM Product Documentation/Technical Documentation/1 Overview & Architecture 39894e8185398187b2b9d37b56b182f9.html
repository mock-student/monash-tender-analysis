1. Overview & Architecture

What is NTU CSM?

The NTU Career Services Management (CSM) platform is the digital platform for Nanyang Technological University's Career & Attachment Office (CAO). It helps the office run its career-coaching programme (SGCC) — organising and tracking coaching sessions between students, staff, and coaches — and communicate with students through email campaigns, forms, and calendar invitations.

  • Production: csm.ntu.edu.sg
  • Region: ap-southeast-1 (Singapore)

All environments run in Singapore (ap-southeast-1), and each has its own isolated infrastructure — database, containers, and Cognito user pool. The QA environment is restricted to the NTU campus network and a secure VPN connection.

Key integrations

  • AWS: Cognito (accounts & sign-in), S3 (file storage), SES (email), Secrets Manager, CloudWatch
  • NTU Map — campus venue lookup · NTU Staff directory
  • CareerAxis — career-data feed · VMock — resume scoring
  • OpenAI — feedback summaries & sentiment · Notion — Help / FAQ content
  • Sentry — error monitoring · PostHog & Google Analytics — usage analytics (web)

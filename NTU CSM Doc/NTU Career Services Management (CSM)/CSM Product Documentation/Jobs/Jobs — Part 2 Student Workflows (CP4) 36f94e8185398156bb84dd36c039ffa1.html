Jobs — Part 2: Student Workflows (CP4)

1. Overview

Part 2 documents student-facing workflows across three role types: Full-Time Jobs (Job Boards), Non-Credit Bearing (NCB) Internships (GE prefix), and Credit-Bearing (CB) Internships (WIE prefix). Job search, offer acceptance, and placement record access are broadly shared across all three types. CB internships introduce workflows with no FT Jobs or NCB equivalent — skills matching, structured assessment submissions, leave applications, and eligibility-gated approval routing — making the CB student experience significantly richer.

Users: Student · CAO Staff (supporting) · WIE Team (supporting) · School Administrator (approval)

4. Prototype Scope for Vetrick

Student screens to design from scratch:

  1. Job / internship search and discovery — filtered search by programme, city, country, business area, and position type; overseas postings searchable with same filters; results list with apply CTA
  1. Job alert notification preferences — enable/disable job search notifications; set frequency (daily or weekly); criteria selection (6.1.1.90.1–90.3)
  1. Bookmarks / favourites — student saves industry or company results as favourites on the portal (6.1.1.124.1)
  1. CV / resume upload — student uploads resume for job applications; feeds both individual application submissions and CV book (6.1.1.89.1)
  1. Application submission and status tracking — student applies for a role; application status tracked through Applied → Invited → Offered → Accepted/Rejected; synchronised to employer-visible status
  1. Offer acceptance flow — student accepts or rejects an offer; uploads signed offer letter; adds rejection remarks (6.2.1.5.6–5.7)
  1. NCB self-source placement — student updates own internship details without validation/approval; system auto-generates Placement Letter; new employer triggers WIE team notification (6.2.1.17.7)
  1. CB application with eligibility check — student applies for an internship opportunity; system runs eligibility check; eligible applications routed to school for approval; ineligible applications surface a reason prompt on screen (6.2.1.17.4)
  1. Skills profile and matching (CB) — student builds a skills profile (AI-suggested or manually entered); views AI-recommended internship opportunities; skill match percentage indicator shown during application (6.2.1.28.1–28.3)
  1. Assessment submissions (CB) — student enters, edits, and submits journals, presentations, and reports; progress tracked with confirmation and reminder emails; Edge-Plus: team leader submits Final Report on behalf of team; non-leader members are auto-marked complete (6.2.1.21.2, 6.2.1.2.4)
  1. Leave application (CB) — student submits internship leave request via the Leave Application module; approval flow configurable by leave type and duration; student tracks status and receives outcome notification (6.2.1.23.1–23.2)
  1. Amend / cancel request — student requests changes to placement fields; tracks routing and approval; receives notification on outcome (6.2.1.19.1, 6.2.1.19.3)
  1. Financial support application — student fills in and submits a financial support form directly in the system; tracks application status (6.2.1.12.1)
  1. Internship Performance Report (student view) — student views and prints their own IPR based on access rights (6.2.1.26.2)
  1. FTP opportunity notification — upon graduation, student receives a notification of Full-Time opportunities from the employer where they previously completed their internship (6.2.1.15.1)

Screens shared between FT Jobs and NCB student flows (design once, parameterise for type):

  1. Application submission and status tracking — core apply → track → outcome flow is the same for FT Jobs and NCB; position type field and downstream approval step differentiate at point of routing (6.1.1.89.1, 6.2.1.5.6)
  1. Overseas internship application — student applies for an overseas internship or job; consistent with local application flow, with geography-specific fields and overseas placement details update (6.2.1.5.2, 6.1.1.91.1)
  1. Offer acceptance — same accept/reject and signed offer letter upload flow across FT Jobs and NCB (6.2.1.5.6–5.7)
  1. Placement records view — student views all placement records (CB and NCB) and application statuses in one place (6.2.1.17.8)
  1. Feedback submission — student submits internship feedback on completion; same customisable form across NCB and CB (6.2.1.10.1)

CB-only student screens (no FT Jobs or NCB equivalent):

  1. Skills matching — AI-powered skills profile and match percentage indicator; CB internship context only (6.2.1.28.1–28.3)
  1. CB eligibility check and school approval routing — eligibility gate with on-screen ineligible prompt; school routing unique to CB (6.2.1.17.4)
  1. Assessment journal / presentation / report submission — structured per-assessment-type entry with progress tracking; Edge-Plus team leader Final Report enforcement (6.2.1.21.2, 6.2.1.2.4)
  1. Leave application and status tracking — student-initiated leave request with configurable approval; period auto-adjustment for CB; no leave module for FT or NCB (6.2.1.23.1–23.2)
  1. Amend / cancel request routing — student change request routed to School and WIE Administrator for approval; specific to CB placement management (6.2.1.19.1)

Jobs — Part 1: Employer Workflows (CP3)

1. Overview

The Jobs module covers the full lifecycle of job and internship opportunity management at NTU. Part 1 documents employer-facing and administrator-facing workflows across three role types: Full-Time Jobs (Job Boards), Non-Credit Bearing (NCB) Internships (GE prefix), and Credit-Bearing (CB) Internships (WIE prefix). CB internships carry significantly more complex workflows — including campaign windows, faculty allocation, assessments, marks integration, and AWO performance risk — than FT jobs or NCB internships, which share broadly similar posting and application structures.

Users: Employer · CAO Staff / IR Consultant · WIE Administrator · School Administrator · Faculty Supervisor · CAO Career Coach

4. Prototype Scope for Vetrick

Employer and admin screens to design from scratch:

  1. Employer account sign-up and approval flow — employer self-registers for the portal; admin reviews and approves or rejects; status dashboard shows account progress and secured interns
  1. Job / internship posting form — employer creates a role with type, duration, location, position details, and overseas-specific fields; IR Consultant receives email trigger for approval of new and amended postings (Job Boards: 6.1.1.92.1)
  1. Application management dashboard (employer-facing) — applicant list with statuses (Applied, Invited, Offered, Accepted, Rejected), eligibility flags, interview invite and result update, offer generation with letter attachment
  1. CB campaign window setup — WIE team configures campaign parameters; sends targeted emails to student groups and employer invitations; controls employer posting window before student release (6.2.1.17.1–17.3)
  1. Faculty allocation UI — admin sets allocation loads, assigns faculty supervisors individually or in bulk, monitors real-time capacity and availability; faculty self-select mode (6.2.1.22.1–22.3)
  1. Assessment template configuration — admin creates, modifies, and publishes templates for journals, presentations, and AWO survey types (6.2.1.21.4)
  1. AWO rating entry and at-risk management — employer inputs AWO score during evaluation; system calculates final score; threshold breach triggers notification to school and student; Placement at Risk view (6.2.1.18.1)
  1. Internship-to-FTP conversion flow — employer inputs Employability Potential rating; conversion offer extended to intern upon graduation; employer dashboard of previously flagged interns (6.2.1.15.1–15.3)
  1. Leave management dashboard (admin) — view and approve student leave requests, track attendance percentage, CB: auto-adjust internship period based on approved leave (6.2.1.23.2–23.4)
  1. Marks generation and MES transfer — admin consolidates raw assessment scores, triggers transfer to Assessment Mark Entry System, mark moderation field for post-release faculty updates (6.2.1.24.1–24.2)
  1. Edge-Plus and WSDEG programme management — Edge-Plus team structure with team leader Final Report enforcement; WSDEG multi-block configuration and auto-assignment (6.2.1.2.1, 6.2.1.3.1–3.2)
  1. CV book compilation — admin view for collecting and exporting students' CV/Resume book (6.1.1.129.1)
  1. Internship Performance Report generation — configurable fields, customisable layout, print/download (6.2.1.26.1)

Screens shared between FT Jobs and NCB internship flows (design once, parameterise for type):

  1. Employer posting form — FT Jobs and NCB internships share similar posting structure; position type field distinguishes role category (6.1.1.92.1, 6.2.1.13.1)
  1. Overseas posting (CAO-initiated) — same admin-side form for posting on behalf of an overseas employer; overseas employer tracks student application records (6.1.1.91.2–91.3, 6.2.1.16.1–16.2)
  1. Bulk application download — shared feature across NCB and CB contexts (6.2.1.5.3)
  1. Placement records view (employer-facing) — employer sees student placement records and application statuses; shared NCB/CB data model (6.2.1.17.8)
  1. Feedback and programme evaluation admin — download student feedback results, trigger evaluation emails; same flow for NCB and CB (6.2.1.10.2, 6.2.1.11.1–11.2)
  1. Reporting dashboard — new roles by industry/employer, new employers by industry, employer events, CAO events; same report structure for Job Boards (6.1.1.97.1) and Internship Boards (6.2.1.25.1)

1. Overview

The Coaching & Appointments module manages the full lifecycle of 1:1 career coaching and consultant sessions, coaching workshops, and briefings between students and their designated Career Coaches or IR Consultants. Students view available timeslots for their assigned coach, request or book sessions, and receive confirmation and calendar invites; coaches set availability, approve requests, log session notes, and mark attendance. Admins manage coach accounts, school assignments, session configuration, and reporting. Several booking flows, calendar views, notification patterns, and attendance mechanisms overlap substantially with the SGCC module — Vetrick should review SGCC designs before designing from scratch for this module.

Users: Students, Career Coaches, IR Consultants, Admins (L4), Managers (L3).

4. Prototype Scope for Vetrick

Screens and flows to design from scratch (no equivalent SGCC design):

  1. Appointment request & approval flow — student requests a session → coach/consultant receives notification → approves or rejects → student notified (SGCC books directly without an approval step)
  1. IR Consultant profile and account setup — distinct from Career Coach profile if roles differ; confirm in discovery
  1. Recurring availability setup for coaches — date-range based recurring slots with per-week flexibility (Daily / Weekly / Monthly / Yearly / Custom)
  1. Coach-to-school assignment and covering coach management — admin UI for assigning and substituting coaches per school and period
  1. Multiple 1:1 session configuration — admin-configurable rules for how many sessions a student may book, and enforcement at booking time
  1. Backdated appointment creation — role-gated flow with manual check-in/out update capability
  1. Coach session notes — private per-session note entry, visible to other coaches with access rights, hidden from students
  1. Admin/user journal entries — separate journal construct if distinct from coach notes (confirm in discovery)
  1. Workshop and briefing management — listing, signup, capacity, and RSVP views (if not handled by Events module — confirm in discovery)
  1. Detailed coaching and workshop reports — full field-set report views for coached students, workshop attendees, and briefing attendees

Screens and flows with SGCC overlap (review SGCC designs first — may need no new design or only minor adaptation):

  1. Student calendar view for timeslot selection — designated coach filter, fully-booked indicator, timeslot selection → SGCC: 6.1.1.51.1, 6.1.1.48.2
  1. Coach availability slot setup — coach inputs available slots visible to assigned students only → SGCC: 6.1.1.48.1, 6.1.1.48.2
  1. Online / Face-to-Face mode selector with venue dropdown → SGCC: 6.1.1.50.1, 6.1.1.50.2
  1. Email confirmation + Outlook calendar (.ics) sync → SGCC: 6.1.1.14.1, 6.1.1.14.2
  1. Configurable reminder notifications (x hours/days, multiple triggers) → SGCC: 6.1.1.15.1, 6.1.1.15.2
  1. Cancellation flow (student or coach-initiated, email notification, calendar removal) → SGCC: 6.1.1.49.5
  1. Attendance marking (Online: auto timestamp; F2F: manual check-in/out button with editable timestamps) → SGCC: 6.1.1.53.1, 6.1.1.53.2
  1. Attendance/coaching report structure — field set closely mirrors SGCC attendance report → SGCC: 6.1.1.99.1

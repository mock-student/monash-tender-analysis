1. SGCC Dashboard — Analytics & Reporting

The requirements in this section cover the analytics and reporting dashboard for the SGCC (Small Group Coaching Clinics) module. While these requirements are scoped to SGCC in the first instance, they may generalise to other reports and dashboards generated across the CSM platform.

Users are able to configure and save one or more personalised Favourite views of the dashboard, allowing them to quickly return to a preferred combination of filters and display settings without reconfiguring each time.

The dashboard includes a custom date filter field, enabling users to define a specific date range for the data displayed. This gives users flexibility to analyse activity across any time period, beyond any default view.

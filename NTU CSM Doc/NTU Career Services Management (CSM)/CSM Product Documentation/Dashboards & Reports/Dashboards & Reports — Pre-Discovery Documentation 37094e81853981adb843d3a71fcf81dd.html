Dashboards & Reports — Pre-Discovery Documentation

1. Overview

The Dashboards & Reports module spans two distinct but related capabilities. Dashboards provide role-personalised, real-time operational views of student activity, placement outcomes, coaching data, and employer interactions — designed for moment-to-moment monitoring and in-platform navigation. Report Writer provides a flexible, exportable reporting engine enabling CAO staff and administrators to customise filter criteria, select fields dynamically, and generate structured data extracts from any module — designed for ad-hoc analysis, compliance reporting, and data exports. Several requirements, particularly the internship report suite (15 named reports), represent the dedicated reporting layer for the Internship Boards (WIE/GE) module and are documented here by milestone assignment.

Users: Students · Employers · Career Coaches · IR Consultants · Faculty Supervisors · MSO/School Administrators · WIE Administrators · CAO Staff · Managers (L3) · Admins (L4)

  1. Customisable dashboard home — user pins/bookmarks module shortcuts, re-orders widgets, resumes saved form drafts from the home screen
  1. Coach and Consultant dashboards — session history, upcoming appointments, attendance tracking, coaching notes summary; confirm with SGCC designs whether these are extensions of the same component (6.1.2.1.8.a–b)
  1. Internship dashboards — Employer and CAO Staff views — Employer: campaign status, current intern list, AWO pending tasks, graduation interest-to-hire list; CAO Staff: placement outcome metrics with drill-down, employer performance ratings, at-risk alerts filterable by college/school/programme (6.2.2.1.2.a)
  1. Internship dashboards — Faculty and MSO/School Admin views — Faculty: placement outcomes scoped to their discipline, during-internship assessment stage tracker with completed stages greyed out; MSO: approval queue for applications, allocation status, placement at risk view (6.2.2.1.2.b, 6.2.2.1.2.d)
  1. Internship dashboard — Student view — pre-registration checklist (eligibility, safety quiz, period registration), visual stages-of-application tracker, during-internship assessment to-do list with greyed completed stages, post-internship performance report access (6.2.2.1.2.e)
  1. Management Admin Dashboard — high-level overview screen; confirm scope and contents in discovery (6.2.2.1.2.c)
  1. Report Writer engine — field selector UI with dynamic dropdowns, multi-criteria filter panel (school, industry, resource type, topic, date range, role), export to CSV/XLS, save and reuse filter combinations (6.1.1.133.1, 6.1.1.134.1, 6.1.1.136.1)
  1. Pre-built report templates (Career Services) — Student Details, Events suite (registrations, attendance, feedback, event list, calendar), Resources/Engagement; confirm whether these are Report Writer presets or standalone pages (6.1.2.1.3.a, 6.1.2.1.4.a–g, 6.1.2.1.5.a, 6.1.2.1.6.c)
  1. Internship report suite (WIE) — 15 named reports: Internship Performance, Allocation Group, Assessment, Assessment Tracking, Eligibility, Faculty Allocation, Opportunity, Placement Tracking, Self-sourced Application, Student Satisfaction Survey, WSDeg lists ×3, Placement at Risk, AWO at Risk; confirm whether generated via Report Writer or module-native (6.2.2.1.3.a–o)
  1. Student Data Snapshot (Manager view) — drill-down student profile showing full cross-module activity: coaching sessions, SGCC, internships, jobs, events, blacklist status (6.1.1.121.1)
  1. External report exports — MOE Life Skills and QS Employer Listing outputs; confirm field structure and delivery format (6.2.2.1.4.a–b)
